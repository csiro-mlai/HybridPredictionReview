Since last time

Dan has been reviewing alternative techniques from the literature for non-stationary PDEs

  • Weak formulation Bao et al. (2020); Zang et al. (2020). This is very clever, but does not solve the problem that it is hard to use the “implicit representation” output, and it is hard to see how it could generalise to multiple problems.
  • Basis decomposition Sigrist, Künsch, and Stahel (2015); Liu, Yeo, and Lu (2020); Li et al. (2020), which is apparently especially useful for advection-diffusion equations. This looks much more useful. The most basic basis choice (Fourier) is probably an easy choice.
